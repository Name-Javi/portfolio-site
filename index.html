About me

Who is this guy?

Avid explorer, gamer, and amateur chef Javier Solis has spent his life pursuing ways to channel his creativity into something tangible. Through years of experience, continuing education, and intense training, the Arkansas native has found his career-focused passion: technology and web development. When he isn’t enjoying spending time with his wife, Jessica, Javier is honing his extensive skill set. Whether it is through tech-based personal research and skill development, or practicing an intense new recipe in the kitchen Javier can be defined by his pursuit of activity and challenge and nearly constant improvement. Intricate and detailed works are the mark of Javier’s mind, easily transforming projects into accomplishments and delicious results. Polyglot and community volunteer Javier surrounds himself with positivity, levity, and radiating joy through engaging in outdoor activities with his close friends and his delightful pup, Dixie.
